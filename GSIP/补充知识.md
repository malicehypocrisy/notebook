# 一、多元正态分布

$$
\mu = 
\begin{bmatrix}
\mu_1 \\
\mu_2
\end{bmatrix} ,
\Sigma = 
\begin{bmatrix}
\Sigma_{11} & \Sigma_{12} \\
\Sigma_{21} & \Sigma_{22}
\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 条件分布的均值向量为 $\mu_1 + \Sigma_{12} \Sigma_{22}^{-1} (a - \mu_2)$
* 条件分布的协方差矩阵为 $\Sigma_{11} - \Sigma_{12} \Sigma_{22}^{-1} \Sigma_{21}$
